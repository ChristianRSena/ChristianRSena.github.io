What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Nobuo Uematsu's impact on the world of video game music is immeasurable, and he continues to be celebrated for his contributions to the industry. His legacy extends beyond gaming, as his compositions have garnered recognition in the broader music community, highlighting the artistic merit of video game music.
